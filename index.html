Our Story

Our journey started nineteen years ago when our first Dormans restaurant opened its doors in Nanyuki. With that flagship store, we set out to bring to life a series of high-quality coffee lounges that offer not only delicious food and exceptional service, but unparalleled ambiance too. Today, we have 10 stores across Kenya, and are one of the only brands to achieve ‘Superbrand’ status in East Africa. More than a restaurant, Dormans Cafe is a lifestyle and a social hub in the buzzing capital of Laikipia County. We are firm believers that dining is about so much more than food. We combine inspirational spaces, quality products, and a sense of community to give you the full experience.
